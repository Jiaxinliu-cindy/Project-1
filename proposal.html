Project Proposal - Jiaxin (Cindy) Liu


About the Website

For the project I have decided to make a memorial website for my dog, Sugar, who passed away about a month ago. This website will be all about Sugar, and her life with us. I want to use text, pictures, and videos to tell her story and everything that she has been through. Ever since she passed away, my family and I have been finding different ways to document her life so I thought this project would be a perfect opportunity for me.

Objectives

  1. The main objective of this website is create an online tribute for Sugar. So, my family and I can have a place to see her when we miss her.

  2. This website is also created for my father. He raised Sugar all by himself for the past two years. When my mom and I were away, it was Sugar and Ginger who kept him company. He treated Sugar like his own daughter so he has been going through a really hard time after Sugar passed away. I want to give this website to him as a gift for his upcoming birthday.

  3. This website will also be used as a coyotes information page for our neighbors and other California residences. Since we live near the coyotes cliff and a few other household's pet dogs' lives were also taken by coyotes. I want to raise awareness through this web page and inform people about the harms coyotes can bring to families so that others would not have to go through the same tragedy we did.

Target Audience

The main target audience would be my family since this is a memorial page for our dog. My secondary target audience would be our neighbors because our properties are located near the field where most coyotes live in. If possible, I would also want to expand my target audiences to the greater areas of California.

Contents

I will have three pages that talk about Sugar's life time. One page will focus on her childhood, when she first came to my family. Another page will document the hard times that she has been through such as, her illnesses and her death. The third page will focus on pictures of my family and her and also picture of her and her sister, Ginger. For the forth page, I want to focus on basic information about coyotes in California, including there habitats, habits, harms and ways to protect pet animals and ourselves when facing coyotes' attacks.
